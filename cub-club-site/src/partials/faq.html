FAQ

Quick answers. No nonsense.

How much is shipping?

Shipping is paid by the customer (PUDO locker-to-locker). Price depends on the PUDO size tier.

When will my order ship?

Orders ship within 24–48 business hours. Orders before 14:00 typically ship next business day.

Do you do home installs?

No. These are DIY kits. Follow the included instructions. For top-of-stairs gates, use hardware-mounted gates.

What if something is missing?

Send your order reference + a photo. We’ll replace missing items quickly.
