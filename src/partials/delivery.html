Delivery (PUDO Lockers)

Collect when it suits you. Shipping is paid by the customer.

How it works

1) Add to cart & checkout
Add items to your cart, then checkout to send us your delivery details.
2) Send your details
Cell number + preferred PUDO locker/area (we can confirm by message).
3) Collect with your PIN
We ship to the locker and you receive an SMS with a pickup PIN.

Shipping cost depends on PUDO size tier. We’ll confirm the correct size based on your order.
